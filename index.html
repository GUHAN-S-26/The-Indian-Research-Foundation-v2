About Us

sys_data.log
> Pioneering Academic Excellence & Innovation

Indian Research Foundation is an ISO-certified research and publication support organization dedicated to academic excellence and innovation. We provide expert support for PhD guidance, Funding proposals, Research & Review Article Writing, Synopsis & Thesis Writing, Book chapter writing, IEEE projects, Data analysis, and Journal Publication.

We empower students, researchers, scholars, and professionals with end-to-end support, including topic selection, proposal development, implementation, technical documentation, and journal submission.

Our mission is to transform innovative ideas into impactful research through high-quality guidance, ethical practices, and global academic standards.

About the Founder

Visionary leadership driving research excellence.

Visionary Leadership

Sunesh N, M.E., Ph.D., PDF

Founder & Research Consultant

Sunesh N is the Founder of Indian Research Foundation (IRF), focusing on providing structured research guidance and academic support to scholars and professionals.

He holds advanced qualifications in engineering and research, with strong expertise in bio-based materials, engineering simulation, and PhD assistance services. He has actively contributed to the research community with 34 SCIE-indexed journal publications, demonstrating his commitment to high-quality and impactful research.

He has extensive experience supporting researchers in thesis development, journal publication processes, and technical documentation, helping scholars achieve successful academic outcomes.

In addition to IRF, he is actively involved in academic training and skill development initiatives, contributing to bridging the gap between research and practical applications.
